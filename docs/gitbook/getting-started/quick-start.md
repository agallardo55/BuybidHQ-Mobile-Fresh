## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [buybidhq.com](https://buybidhq.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right
3. Choose your plan:
   - **Beta (Free)** - Perfect for getting started
   - **Connect Premium** - Includes Market View
   - **Annual** - Best value at $50/month

4. Fill in your information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Mobile number (for MFA security)
   - Password (8+ characters)
   - Dealership name

5. Check the box to agree to SMS notifications
6. Click **Create Account**

{% hint style="success" %}
**Success!** You'll be redirected to your dashboard.
{% endhint %}

---

## Step 2: Add Your First Buyer

Before you can send bid requests, you need at least one buyer in your network.

1. Click **Buyers** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Add Buyer** in the top right
3. Enter buyer information:
   - **Name:** Buyer's full name
   - **Company:** Their business name
   - **Mobile Number:** Where they'll receive bid requests
   - **Email:** (optional) For additional contact

4. Click **Save**

{% hint style="info" %}
**Pro Tip:** You can add buyers to groups later for faster selection when sending bids.
{% endhint %}

---

## Step 3: Create Your First Bid Request

Now let's send your first bid!

1. Click **Dashboard** or **Create Bid Request**
2. In the Vehicle Information section, either:
   - **Option A:** Enter the VIN and click **Decode** (recommended)
   - **Option B:** Manually select year, make, model, trim

3. Add vehicle photos (5-10 recommended):
   - Click **Add Photos** or drag & drop
   - Include exterior, interior, and any damage

4. Set vehicle condition and mileage:
   - Choose condition (Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor)
   - Enter mileage

5. Select buyers:
   - Check the buyer you just added
   - Or select a buyer group

6. Review and click **Send Bid Request**
