## Upgrading Your Plan

### From Beta to Premium

**To Upgrade:**
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select **Connect Premium** or **Annual**
4. Enter payment information
5. Click **Confirm Upgrade**

**What Happens:**
- ‚úÖ Immediate access to premium features
- ‚úÖ Prorated billing (credit for unused time)
- ‚úÖ Next billing on your new cycle
- ‚úÖ Email confirmation sent

---

### From Monthly to Annual

**Switch to Annual:**

**Billing:**

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

**To Add Card:**
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Billing** ‚Üí **Payment Method**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter card details:
   - Card number
   - Expiration date (MM/YY)
   - CVV (3-4 digits)
   - Billing ZIP code
4. Click **Save**

**Accepted Cards:**
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

---

### Updating Your Card

**When to Update:**
- Card expired
- Lost or stolen card
- New card number
- Billing address changed

**To Update:**
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Billing** ‚Üí **Payment Method**
2. Click **Update**
3. Enter new card details
4. Click **Save**

**Note:** Changes apply immediately. Next charge uses new card.

---

### Removing a Payment Method

**Cannot Remove if:**
- It's your only payment method
- You have an active paid subscription

**Must:**
- Add new payment method first, OR
- Downgrade to Beta (free) plan

## Failed Payments

### When a Payment Fails

**Common Reasons:**
- Expired card
- Insufficient funds
- Card blocked by bank
- Incorrect billing ZIP

**What Happens:**
1. Email notification sent immediately
2. Automatic retry in 3 days
3. Second retry in 7 days
4. Account downgraded to Beta if still failing

---

### Resolving Failed Payments

**To Fix:**
1. Check email for failure notice
2. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Billing**
3. Update payment method
4. Click **Retry Payment**

**Or:**
- Contact your bank/card issuer
- Ensure card has sufficient funds
- Verify billing address is correct

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change plans mid-cycle?**
A: Yes. You'll be prorated for unused time on your current plan.

**Q: What happens if my card is declined?**
A: We'll email you immediately and retry in 3 days. Update card to avoid service interruption.

**Q: Can I get a discount for multiple users?**
A: Not currently. One price covers unlimited team members on all plans.

**Q: Do you offer nonprofit or education discounts?**
A: Email support@buybidhq.com with proof of status. We review case-by-case.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover. No PayPal, crypto, or ACH currently.

**Q: Can I pay annually with monthly invoices?**
A: No. Annual plan is charged once per year ($599). Monthly plan is $99/month.
