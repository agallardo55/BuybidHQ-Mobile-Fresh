# Viewing Bid Responses

Track and manage buyer offers from your dashboard.

---

## Overview

When buyers respond to your bid requests, you can view and compare all offers in one place to make informed decisions.

**Available On:** All plans (Beta, Connect, Annual)
**Required Role:** Member or higher

---

## Accessing Bid Responses

### From the Dashboard

1. Log in to [app.buybidhq.com](https://app.buybidhq.com)
2. Your **Dashboard** shows all active bid requests
3. Look for bid requests with response indicators:
   - **New badge** - Unread responses
   - **Number badge** - Total response count (e.g., "3 offers")

---

### Opening a Bid Request

1. Click on any bid request card in your dashboard
2. The bid request detail page opens
3. You'll see:
   - Vehicle information
   - All photos
   - Delivery status
   - List of buyers contacted
   - All responses received

## Viewing Offers

### Offer Details

Each response includes:

**Buyer Information:**
- Buyer name
- Company name
- Contact details (mobile, email)

**Offer Details:**
- Offer amount (e.g., "$15,500")
- Timestamp (when submitted)
- Additional comments or questions

**Quick Actions:**
- Call buyer (click phone number)
- Email buyer (click email)
- Mark as accepted/rejected

---

### Comparing Offers

**Sorting Options:**
- **Highest to Lowest** - See best offers first (default)
- **Lowest to Highest** - See all offers
- **Most Recent** - See newest responses first
- **Oldest First** - See responses in order received

{% hint style="success" %}
**Pro Tip:** The highest offer is automatically highlighted in green for easy identification.
{% endhint %}

---

## Taking Action on Offers

### Accepting an Offer

1. Review all offers
2. Click **Accept** on your chosen offer
3. Buyer is marked as "Accepted" (visible only to you)
4. Contact buyer directly to finalize sale

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important:** BuybidHQ doesn't handle payment processing. You'll need to coordinate payment and pickup with the buyer directly.
{% endhint %}

---

### Rejecting an Offer

1. Find the offer to reject
2. Click **Reject** or **Decline**
3. Offer is marked as "Rejected" (visible only to you)
4. Optionally add a reason for your records

**When to Reject:**
- Offer too low
- Already accepted another offer
- Buyer not responsive
- Vehicle sold elsewhere

## Response Notifications

### How You're Notified

When a buyer responds, you receive:

**Dashboard Notification:**
- Red badge on bid request card
- "New offer" indicator

**Email Notification** (if enabled):
- Email with offer summary
- Direct link to view full response

**SMS Notification** (if enabled):
- Text message with buyer name and offer amount
- Link to view in dashboard

**Enable/Disable Notifications:**
1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Notifications**
3. Toggle email/SMS preferences

## No Responses Yet?

### What to Do

**If no responses within 1 hour:**

1. **Check delivery status** - Did SMS actually deliver?
2. **Follow up with buyers** - Call your top 2-3 buyers directly
3. **Review your pricing** - Is the vehicle condition/price competitive?
4. **Send to more buyers** - Add to your buyer network or send to additional groups

**Common Reasons for Low Response:**

‚ùå **Poor photo quality** - Buyers can't assess condition
‚ùå **Unrealistic pricing** - Price doesn't match condition/market
‚ùå **Wrong buyer selection** - Sent to buyers who don't specialize in that vehicle type
‚ùå **Timing issues** - Sent after hours or on weekends
‚ùå **Vehicle condition concerns** - Visible damage without explanation

## Bid Request History

### Viewing Past Requests

1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Click **Filter** dropdown
3. Select filter options:
   - **All Requests** - Everything
   - **Active** - Awaiting responses
   - **Completed** - Sale finalized
   - **Expired** - No responses after 7 days
   - **Archived** - Manually archived

---

### Searching Past Bids

**Search by:**
- Vehicle VIN
- Year, make, model
- Buyer name
- Date range
- Response count

**Example Searches:**
- "2020 Honda Accord" - Find all Honda Accords from 2020
- "Smith Auto" - Find all requests sent to Smith Auto
- "No responses" - Find requests with zero offers

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I delete a response?**
A: No. All responses are preserved for record-keeping. You can mark them as rejected.

**Q: Can buyers see other buyers' offers?**
A: No. Each buyer only sees their own response. Offers are confidential.

**Q: What if a buyer submits multiple offers?**
A: Both offers show. The most recent offer is highlighted. Contact buyer to clarify.

**Q: How long do responses stay visible?**
A: Forever. All bid history is preserved unless you delete the entire bid request.

**Q: Can I edit a buyer's offer?**
A: No. Offers are locked once submitted. Contact buyer directly to negotiate.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See Responses

**Problem:** Dashboard shows responses, but can't view them

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try different browser
4. Check internet connection
5. Contact support if persistent

---

### Response Notifications Not Working

**Problem:** Not receiving email/SMS notifications for new offers

**Solutions:**
1. Check **Account Settings** ‚Üí **Notifications**
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Check spam/junk folder
4. Verify mobile number for SMS
5. Ensure notifications are enabled
