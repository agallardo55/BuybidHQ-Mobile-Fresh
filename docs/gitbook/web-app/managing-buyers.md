# Managing Buyers

Learn how to add, organize, and manage your wholesale buyer network in BuybidHQ.

---

## Overview

Your buyer network is the foundation of BuybidHQ. Organize buyers efficiently to send bid requests faster and build stronger wholesale relationships.

**Available On:** All plans (Beta, Connect, Annual)
**Required Role:** Member or higher

---

## Adding Buyers

### Adding Your First Buyer

1. Click **Buyers** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Add Buyer** button (top right)
3. Fill in buyer information:

**Required Fields:**
- **Name:** Buyer's full name
- **Mobile Number:** Where they'll receive SMS bid requests (formatted automatically)

**Optional Fields:**
- **Company:** Business name
- **Email:** Additional contact method
- **Notes:** Internal notes (not visible to buyer)

4. Click **Save**

{% hint style="success" %}
**Success!** Your buyer is now in your network and ready to receive bid requests.
{% endhint %}

---

### Adding Multiple Buyers

**For bulk import:**
1. Click **Buyers**
2. Click **Import Buyers** (top right)
3. Download CSV template
4. Fill in buyer information
5. Upload completed CSV
6. Review and confirm

**CSV Format:**
```csv
name,mobile_number,company,email
John Smith,(555) 123-4567,Smith Auto,john@smithauto.com
Jane Doe,(555) 987-6543,Doe Motors,jane@doemotors.com
```

{% hint style="info" %}
**Pro Tip:** Export your existing contact list to CSV, then import to BuybidHQ!
{% endhint %}

## Organizing Buyer Groups

Buyer groups allow you to send bid requests to multiple buyers with one click.

### Creating a Buyer Group

1. Go to **Buyers** page
2. Click **Groups** tab
3. Click **Create Group**
4. Name your group (e.g., "Auction Buyers", "Direct Buyers")
5. Select buyers to include
6. Click **Save Group**

### Suggested Group Organization

**By Buyer Type:**
- Auction Buyers
- Direct Buyers
- Retail Buyers
- Wholesale Partners

**By Vehicle Specialization:**
- Luxury Buyers
- Domestic Buyers
- Import Buyers
- Truck/SUV Buyers

**By Region:**
- Local Buyers
- Regional Buyers
- National Buyers

**By Frequency:**
- Primary Buyers
- Secondary Buyers
- Occasional Buyers

{% hint style="success" %}
**Pro Tip:** Create multiple groups and assign buyers to several groups based on different criteria.
{% endhint %}

## Editing Buyer Information

### Update a Single Buyer

1. Go to **Buyers** page
2. Find the buyer (use search if needed)
3. Click the **Edit** icon (pencil)
4. Update information
5. Click **Save**

**What You Can Edit:**
- Name
- Mobile number
- Company
- Email
- Internal notes

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important:** If you change a mobile number, future bid requests will go to the new number. Past requests remain unchanged.
{% endhint %}

---

### Deactivating vs. Deleting Buyers

**Deactivate a Buyer:**
- Buyer remains in your list
- Won't appear in buyer selection
- Can reactivate anytime
- Preserves bid history

**Delete a Buyer:**
- Permanently removes buyer
- Cannot undo
- Bid history remains but shows "Deleted Buyer"

**To Deactivate:**
1. Edit buyer
2. Toggle **Active** switch to OFF
3. Save

**To Delete:**
1. Edit buyer
2. Click **Delete Buyer**
3. Confirm deletion

{% hint style="info" %}
**Recommendation:** Deactivate instead of delete to preserve relationships and data.
{% endhint %}

## Common Questions

**Q: Is there a limit to how many buyers I can add?**
A: No limit on any plan. Add unlimited buyers.

**Q: Can buyers see other buyers in my network?**
A: No. Buyers only see their own information and the vehicles you send them.

**Q: Can I export my buyer list?**
A: Yes. Click **Export** on the Buyers page to download CSV.

**Q: What happens if a buyer's phone is disconnected?**
A: SMS will fail to deliver. Check delivery status and update their number.

**Q: Can I share buyer groups with team members?**
A: Yes, if you're on a team account. All users see the same buyer list and groups.

**Q: How do I prevent accidentally sending to the wrong buyer?**
A: Double-check selected buyers before clicking "Send" on each bid request.

## Troubleshooting

### Buyer Not Receiving SMS

**Problem:** Buyer says they didn't receive bid request

**Solutions:**
1. Check delivery status in bid request details
2. Verify mobile number is correct (10 digits)
3. Ask buyer to check spam/blocked numbers
4. Confirm buyer has SMS-capable phone
5. Try sending a test bid request

[Full SMS troubleshooting ‚Üí](../troubleshooting/sms-issues.md)

---

### Cannot Delete Buyer

**Problem:** Delete button is grayed out

**Possible Reasons:**
- Buyer has pending bid requests
- You don't have permission (Member role)
- System error

**Solutions:**
- Wait for pending bids to complete
- Contact Account Admin for deletion
- Deactivate buyer instead
